---
title: 选择即改变
order: 3
---

添加`changeOnSelect`属性，即可使组件选择即改变`value`，这样可以只选中父级

```vdt
import {Cascader} from 'kpc';

<div>
    <Cascader data={this.get('data')} v-model="value" changeOnSelect />
    You selected: {JSON.stringify(this.get('value'))}
</div>
```
